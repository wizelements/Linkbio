# Monetization Strategy

## MVP Revenue Model (Start Now)

### Pricing
- **Basic (Free):** 1 page, basic analytics
- **Professional ($9/page/month):** Unlimited pages, advanced features

## Sales Process (Day 1)

### 1. Create Your Own Landing Pages
- Create test pages for your business
- Get familiar with the admin
- Build 5 example pages

### 2. Manual Outreach (Day 1-3)
Target: Farmers market vendors, small service providers, creators

**Where to find them:**
- Local farmers markets
- Instagram business profiles
- Facebook business pages
- Nextdoor/neighborhood apps
- Google My Business listings

**Your pitch:**
> "I created beautiful landing pages that convert visitors to customers. Takes 5 minutes to set up, includes lead capture and analytics. Just $9/month."

**Demo:**
- Show them live example at `linkbio.com/john-doe`
- Let them see the form (lead capture)
- Show analytics dashboard

### 3. Sign First Customer
1. Go to `/admin`
2. Create their page (5 minutes)
3. Send them link: `linkbio.com/<their-slug>`
4. Collect payment (manual: Stripe Link, PayPal, Venmo)

### 4. Upsell Path
Once they have 1 page:
- "Want another page for your second business? Still $9/month"
- "Add custom domain? +$5/month"
- "Email notifications when you get leads? +$3/month"

## Payment Integration (Simple Path)

### Phase 1: Manual (Right Now)
1. Create page in admin
2. Send customer Stripe Link
3. Get paid manually
4. Mark as paid in your records

**Tool:** Stripe Links (no code)
**Setup time:** 5 minutes
**Revenue start:** Today

### Phase 2: Automated Stripe
1. Generate payment link per page
2. Link to Stripe API
3. Auto-create account on payment success
4. Email customer their page URL

**Setup time:** 4 hours
**When:** End of Week 1

### Phase 3: Customer Accounts
1. Customer creates account
2. Creates own pages
3. Subscribe to unlimited pages plan
4. Complete self-serve

**Setup time:** 2 days
**When:** End of Week 2

## FAQ for Customers

**Q: How long does it take to set up?**
A: 5 minutes. You tell us your details, we deploy your page.

**Q: Can I customize the design?**
A: Yes (Phase 2). For now, it's a professional template.

**Q: Do I own my page?**
A: You own the content. Your page lives on our platform.

**Q: Can I move to my own domain?**
A: Yes. Later versions support custom domains (+$5/month).

**Q: What if I stop paying?**
A: Page goes down. All leads are yours to download.
